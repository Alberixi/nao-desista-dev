┌──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│  ABSTRACT                                                    │
└──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘

This paper presents a comprehensive framework for digital
forensic analysis, incorporating traditional methodologies with
modern machine learning techniques. The proposed framework
addresses the challenges of increasing data volumes and
encryption in modern investigations.

Keywords: Digital Forensics, Machine Learning, Evidence Analysis

┌──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│  1. INTRODUCTION                                             │
└──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘

Digital forensics has become increasingly critical in modern
investigations. With the proliferation of digital devices and
encrypted communications, traditional forensic methods often
fall short. This paper introduces a hybrid approach combining
established forensic procedures with machine learning algorithms.

│  5. DISCUSSION                                               │

The integration of machine learning techniques with traditional
forensic methods shows significant improvement in both accuracy
and efficiency. However, challenges remain in:

┌──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│  • Admissibility of ML-generated evidence in court          │
│  • Explainability of ML decisions                           │
│  • Training data quality and bias                           │
│  • Computational requirements                               │
└──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘
